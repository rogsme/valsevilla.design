One of my key contributions was **redesigning the navigation system for Deals**. The previous flow made it difficult for users to understand where they were and how to move between discovery, solution, and proposal stages. By analyzing user behavior and gathering feedback, I identified the need to simplify the patterns and create **clearer paths**.

The redesign focused on:

- Establishing a **consistent navigation pattern** across all deal stages.
- Designing a structure that feels **intuitive for experienced users** while remaining **friendly for new sales reps**.
- Improving the **information hierarchy** so users could focus on what truly matters in progressing a deal.

This resulted in a more **predictable, user-friendly experience** that reduces friction, shortens onboarding time for new reps, and makes navigating deals more efficient.

### Problem

Revolear's early AI features — like **AI tags information hidden in tooltips** — weren't providing enough value to users. Information felt scattered, navigation across deals was unclear, and senior reps were spending excessive time teaching new reps how to use the platform effectively.

![Before](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64a221aaa84be0a10e31c5a4/68dbdc28535fc4d32827122d_before.webp)

### Approach

I initiated a redesign starting with a simple curiosity-driven concept: *what if AI insights were displayed in a more helpful, actionable way?*

Clearly the AI had lots of information but the human brain doesn't work the same way — we need structure and a way to scan and find easily what we need.

![After — first version to kickoff the conversation](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64a221aaa84be0a10e31c5a4/68dbd3537f72f4c7a83ec6b7_After.webp)

From there, we:

- Conducted **research and brainstorming sessions** to understand user needs.
- Focused on both **experienced sales reps** and **newer reps** who required clearer guidance.
- Identified that the true challenge was **onboarding and reducing training time**, not just automation.

### Solution

We reshaped the platform with a **user-friendly, AI-first design**:

**Navigation Redesign** — Introduced a step-by-step structure (Discovery → Solution → Strategy → Engagement) plus supporting sections like Workbench, Dashboard, Settings, and a CTA to the Briefing Room.

![Top navigation bar](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64a221aaa84be0a10e31c5a4/68dbd9a240cec4b5ce849eb5_Top%20Nav%20bar%20(2).webp)

**AI-First Sidebar** — Transformed the sidebar from a basic communication tool into the **main AI interaction hub**, enabling contextual comments, guidance, and faster collaboration.

![AI-first sidebar](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64a221aaa84be0a10e31c5a4/68dbd9b5474d4ecf57279359_sidebar%20(1).webp)

**AI as the Backbone** — Leveraged memory and historical context to suggest the best outcomes, making the workflow smoother for seniors and intuitive for new reps.

![AI as the backbone](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/64a221aaa84be0a10e31c5a4/68dbd9c5394aafe7cc8a674c_AI%20as%20the%20backbone%20(2).webp)

### Impact

- **Simplified onboarding**: Reduced reliance on senior reps for training.
- **Clearer deal navigation**: Sales reps follow predictable, guided steps.
- **Smarter workflows**: AI delivers context-aware recommendations, saving time and improving outcomes.
- **Stronger collaboration**: Direct, in-context AI interactions replaced siloed communication.
