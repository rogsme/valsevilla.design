## Overview

Apricity is a wellness and mental health application designed to help users build and maintain positive daily habits. The project focused on creating an intuitive, calming experience that encourages daily engagement without feeling overwhelming.

## The Challenge

Users often struggle to maintain wellness routines due to app fatigue and overwhelming feature sets. The challenge was to create a simple yet engaging experience that fits naturally into daily life.

## My Role

As the lead product designer, I was responsible for user research, interaction design, and the complete UI design system. I worked closely with the development team to ensure design feasibility and quality implementation.

## The Process

- Conducted user interviews with 15+ participants to understand wellness habits
- Created user personas and journey maps
- Designed wireframes and interactive prototypes
- Ran usability testing sessions to validate design decisions
- Iterated based on feedback and analytics

## The Solution

A clean, minimalist interface with gentle color palettes and micro-interactions that make daily check-ins feel rewarding rather than burdensome. Key features include habit tracking, mood journaling, and guided mindfulness exercises.
