## Overview

Rise is a fintech application designed to make personal finance management accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial literacy level. The app simplifies budgeting, savings, and investment tracking.

## The Challenge

Financial apps often overwhelm users with complex data and jargon, creating barriers for people who want to take control of their finances but don't know where to start.

## My Role

I served as the lead UX/UI designer, responsible for user research, the complete design system, and ensuring the experience felt approachable rather than intimidating.

## The Process

- Researched financial literacy barriers through user interviews
- Analyzed competing fintech apps for strengths and gaps
- Developed progressive disclosure patterns for complex financial data
- Designed an accessible color system and typography hierarchy
- Conducted A/B testing on key onboarding flows

## The Solution

An approachable interface that uses clear visualizations, plain language, and progressive disclosure to help users understand their finances. The design gradually introduces more advanced features as users build confidence.
