## Description & Target Audience

Rise is a financial technology solution built for new business owners to collect, spend, and manage money. Designed to help to:

- Spend less
- Track transactions
- Manage card spending
- Cashback on every purchase

![Cashback](/images/projects/rise/cashback.png)

## Cashback

Rise believes in returning one part of what users give to them, for that reason one of the plus for the users is that they receive 1% of every purchase they make

## Design process

### Research

Rise believes in returning one part of what users give to them, for that reason one of the plus for the users is that they receive 1% of every purchase they make

### Ideation

The best way to present a product like this is to build the trust between the user and the product. The color scheme is going to be according to this emotions that we want to evoke, and the system transparency, maintaining the users informed about the system status

### Design

Not only the transparency is going to play an important role, the UX writing, usability, and having an easy way to find things is going to be important to build the trust of our users.

> In the realm of fintech, user experience is the compass guiding innovation while creating a trustworthy, intuitive, and visually engaging financial tool.
