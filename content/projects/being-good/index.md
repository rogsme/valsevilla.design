## Overview

Being Good is a social impact platform designed to connect volunteers with community organizations and meaningful opportunities to make a difference in their local communities.

## The Challenge

Many people want to volunteer but find it difficult to discover opportunities that match their skills, interests, and availability. Existing platforms often feel impersonal and lack engagement.

## My Role

As the product designer, I handled end-to-end design from initial research through final UI delivery, including branding and the design system.

## The Process

- Conducted stakeholder interviews with nonprofit organizations
- Surveyed potential volunteers about their motivations and barriers
- Mapped the volunteer journey from discovery to ongoing engagement
- Created wireframes and interactive prototypes
- Tested with both volunteers and organization administrators

## The Solution

A warm, inviting platform that makes finding volunteer opportunities as easy as browsing social media. Features include skill-based matching, flexible scheduling, impact tracking, and community stories that inspire continued engagement.
